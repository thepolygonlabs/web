Hi, I'm Neo Owen

Meet me & my friends & the entire community

PolygonLabs is a place for me and my friends and the whole community to come together with experiment, fun and exciting ideas that can be realized in the future.

Are we talking about metaverse? Yes for sure.

At Polygonlabs we believe anyone has an interesting and brilliant idea. Adapting NFT in the metaverse is one of the reasons why this lab was created. There still needs a lot of research to be done to make the metaverse a place not only for fun, but as a place that has a healthy and safe environment.

The projects we run are not centralized; we prefer the decentralized concept.

Metaverse should be decentralized celebrating various opinions and views that are not subject to any rules & law that only give interest to only one party. We also open up opportunities for more people to participate in its success.

So once again, we are opening up opportunities for many to participate in this polygonlabs project. May it make the world of the future more fun and exciting.
