Introduction

The virtual world is fast becoming a significant area of interest, not just for crypto enthusiasts but all industries. The metaverse presents itself as an alternate world that exists online and promises many opportunities.

As we prepare to move into the metaverse, we should ask ourselves what we want that new reality to look like, and perhaps more importantly, how it should be run.

Idea

What does a boarding pass do in the real world? Well, we often use boarding passes in airlines. If you do not have a boarding pass, that means you are not allowed to get onto the flight.

Boarding Pass DAO NFT is not just an ordinary ticket; it can be uses and integrated with Game and Metaverse. In the metaverse, Boarding Pass DAO NFT is used to connect your characters and determine where your metaverse journey begins. There are only 90,000 Boarding Pass DAO NFT maximum supplies.

Apart from the fact that a Boarding Pass DAO NFT contains all the information that you need to start and begin your journey in the game or metaverse, the Boarding Pass DAO NFT owner also has the privilege to govern the game and metaverse well through the Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO).

Decentralized Metaverse

Where is the fun of the metaverse if it is still centralized and controlled by an organization, company or an authority? It's like we always get banned on social media for breaking community standards. For example, someone may wish to express a view regarding the highly debuted issues which is Covid-19. However the message is blocked and banned by the centralized party because the information provided is allegedly inaccurate and incorrect. But which information is actually accurate? Wouldn't it be better to allow people to express their difference in a polite manner without discriminating against those who have a different opinion?

In centralized world, rules and laws are often used by interested parties to protect themselves. This is where the idea of a decentralized metaverse comes from. The world without ‘rules’, because rules limits our voice and creativity.

DAO in Metaverse

What is DAO

A DAO, or “Decentralized Autonomous Organization” is a community-led entity with no central authority. It is fully autonomous and transparent: smart contracts lay the foundational rules, execute the agreed upon decisions, and at any point, proposals, voting, and even the very code itself can be publicly audited.

In metaverse a DAO is governed entirely by its individual members who own Boarding Pass DAO NFT collectively make critical decisions about the future of the project, such as technical upgrades and treasury allocations.

The Rarity

Even though boarding pass just includes the number, but it is rarely between each others.

For example if you have the same flight with your friend, everything like date, time, terminal, gate, flight, class is the same but definitely not your name and seats, right?

The boarding time, date, flight number, status, seat, gate, terminal e.t.c. is generated randomly with an id that we call matrix, and each matrix has a unique different code.

The rarity can also be associated with some event, such as the first internet being launched on 1 January 1983 or the beginning of the Bitcoin network on 3 January 2009.
